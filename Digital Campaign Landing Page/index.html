Handmade Boots Since 1922

Our Story began in 1922 when our founder, M.L. Leddy, bought the saddle and boot shop he had been working at in Brady, Texas, and ran it with his brothers. Together, they maintained the Leddy craftmanship standard unseen anywhere else.
The business was eventually passed down to Leddy’s sons and son-in-law, then to his grandson, and is now being run by the fourth generation of the family.
Our tradition of handmaking boots and saddles has remained, and we have even expanded our expertise to include custom hats, clothing, belts, buckles, and jewelry.
We take pride in being a reliable, old-fashioned, tried and true Western retailer that Texans can trust. We hope to see you in one of our stores soon so you can become part of the legendary Leddy’s family!
